: Rules Of Simon Game :

  • Press any key from your keyboard to start the game 👆
  • At Level 1 you have to press a particular key which blinks for a moment. After pressing that key an another key also toggle 😉
  • At Level 2 you have to press the first two keys and automatically an another key also toggle 😉😉
  • Continue this process and level up of your game 👍🔥
  • If you press a wrong key 👎 then again press any key from your keyboard to continue the game ✨
